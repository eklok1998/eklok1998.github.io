Where it all started...

Born in a teenage girl's bedroom in 2015, Kay Cosmetics began as a small online shop that strove to bring the fun and shiny back in the matte make-up world. Glitter is our passion; we believe that everyone deserves a sparkle to their day (or a fun night out!), and where better than to put that sparkle than on yourselves?

Since then, we have skyrocketed into an international business that delivers quality, gorgeous products to fifty countries around the world. We are renowned for our eye glitters - safe, eco-friendly, and cruelty-free! Our glitters have been used on runways by the likes of Alexander McQueen, Gucci, and Chanel - with more appearances to come.
